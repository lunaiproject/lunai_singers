Terms of Use for LUNAI Project Voice Models
(Last updated: January 16, 2026)

1. General Provisions
1.1. Voice models and characters of LUNAI Project are intellectual property of the project and their rights holders (voice providers, artists, developers).
1.2. Use of voice models is permitted only in compliance with this agreement. Violation of the rules may result in restricted access to our resources and legal liability.

2. Permitted Use
2.1. Non-commercial use:
- Creation of covers, original songs, fanart, animations, and other creative works is allowed provided that the voice model is credited in the format:
"Character Name" from LUNAI Project
(e.g.: Llane Crow from LUNAI Project).
2.2. Commercial use:
- Written permission from LUNAI Project team is required. To request permission, please send an email to: lunaiproject@gmail.com
Your request must include:
1. Character name (e.g., Llane Crow).
2. Project description (song lyrics/script where the model will be used).
3. Purpose of use (e.g., YouTube monetization, track sales).
The request will be processed within 7 business days.

2.3. 18+ content guidelines:
- Creation of 18+ restricted content is allowed only for the following characters:
Llane Crow, Keiro, Nero, Amaboshi Cipher, Liam Thorne, Karasu Yuutsukoe, Kuro Bousuku, Albus Korde, Gahata Meiji, Allen Crow, Ohne Kuno.

Strictly prohibited:
1. Violence and cruelty:
 - Realistic depictions of bloodshed, dismemberment, or torture.
 - Graphic scenes of physical/psychological abuse.
 - Promotion of self-harm or suicide.
2. Socially harmful content:
 - Racist, xenophobic, or discriminatory materials.
 - Promotion of extremist ideologies.
 - Content justifying violence or crimes against humanity.
3. Characters and project reputation:
 - Depicting characters in humiliating or degrading situations.
 - Scenes contradicting established character lore.
 - Using project symbolism in illegal activities.

LUNAI Project team reserves the exclusive right to determine what materials constitute violations of these rules, including cases not explicitly described in this document.

2.4. Prohibited Forms of Use:
- The creation of interactive chatbots, AI assistants, role-playing models for services similar to Character.AI, or any other forms of interactive communication where a user can engage in a dialogue as or with a LUNAI Project character is prohibited.
- The use of characters as participants in role-playing chats (role-playing games) outside of scenarios approved by the LUNAI Project team is prohibited.

3. Prohibited Actions
3.1. Voice model usage:
- Porting models to other engines (e.g., RVC, Character.AI, UTAU) or modifying them.
- Training third-party AI models using derived audio files or LUNAI Project's original datasets.
- Creating or distributing "voice packs" (including remix samples).
- Using models for fake statements or fraudulent activities.
3.2. Content restrictions:
- Prohibited:
- Racism, xenophobia, incitement to violence.
- Discrediting real individuals or organizations.
- Using characters in illegal activities (e.g., deepfake).
3.3. Technical prohibitions:
- Reverse engineering, decompiling, or bypassing voice model protections.
- Distributing models or their copies.
3.4. Use of Official Illustrations:
- Any commercial use of official LUNAI Project illustrations and visual materials (character artwork, logos, branding) is strictly prohibited without written permission.
- The use of official illustrations and any other project visual materials for training artificial intelligence (AI), including but not limited to generative image models and neural networks, is prohibited.

4. Special Conditions for Voice Providers
4.1. Voice providers retain rights to their voice data. LUNAI Project uses them only under signed agreements.
4.2. Using voice providers' recordings for purposes unrelated to the project (e.g., training third-party models without permission) is prohibited.

5. Liability and Support
5.1. LUNAI Project is not responsible for:
- Quality of user-created content.
- Rule violations by third parties (e.g., unauthorized model distribution).
- Legal consequences arising from users' infringement of third-party rights.
5.2. For dispute resolution, please contact us.

6. Final Provisions
6.1. These rules may be updated. The current version is always available at: https://lunaiproject.github.io/termsofuse/
6.2. Using voice models implies agreement with these terms.
6.3. Final interpretation of these rules and violation assessments are made exclusively by LUNAI Project team.
